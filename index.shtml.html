About me

I currently serve as an Assistant Professor with the VCC group at the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University. I earned my Ph.D at Xidian University in 2024, and obtained my B.Sc from the same university in 2018. My Ph.D advisor was Prof. Chen Bo.

My long-term research goal focuses on developing reliable AI systems to understand our complex and uncertain world.

  • Probabilistic machine learning & Bayesian analysis: Variational inference, hierarchical models, and matrix factorization.
  • Generative models: Diffusion-based models, auto-regressive models.
  • Multimodal representation learning: Vision-language models, multimodal large language models.

We are interested in designing state-of-the-art algorithms and applying these techniques to various applications, including computer vision, natural language processing, and domain alignment. We hope the methods developed within our group promote the development of AGI and improve the richness of our lives.
