About me

Welcome to my academic page!

I’m a machine learning reseacher interested in probabilistic machine learning and deep learning with applications in language and vision.

I obtained my PhD from the Department of Data Science and AI of Monash University in 2019 and then worked as a research fellow at the department until mid 2022.
